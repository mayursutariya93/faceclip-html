Best Practices
  • Face the camera directly with good lighting on your face
  • Use minimal hand gestures and natural head movements
  • Speak naturally with clear pronunciation
  • Avoid rapid movements or excessive gesturing
  • Add pauses [2s] for dramatic effect
  • Avoid wearing reflective glasses or busy patterns
Sample Avatar Video
Movement Tips

Natural movements create better avatars, but too much movement can reduce quality.

  • Small head movements are good (nodding, slight, turns)
  • Keep hand gestures minimal and slow
  • Maintain a consistent distance from the camera
  • If standing, stay within the frame at all times
